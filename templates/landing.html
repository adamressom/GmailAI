How it works

Three steps to inbox clarity

01
🔐

Connect Gmail

Sign in with your Google account. GmailAI reads your emails securely using the official Gmail API — read-only access, no sending.

02
🤖

AI organizes everything

GPT-4o-mini reads each email and sorts it into Career, Resources, Opportunities, or Job categories — with a priority score and summary.

Features

Everything students need

📂

Smart categorization

AI groups emails into Career, Resources, Opportunities, and Job — not by keyword, but by understanding the actual content and intent of each message.

🎯

Priority scoring

Every email gets a priority score from 1–10 based on urgency, deadlines, and opportunity value.

📝

AI summaries

Each email gets a one-sentence summary so you can scan a full category in seconds.

🔗

Cited answers

Every AI response links back to the exact emails it used — no hallucinations, full transparency.

🔒

Read-only Gmail access

GmailAI never sends emails, never modifies your inbox. It uses Google's official OAuth with read-only Gmail scope. Your data stays yours.
